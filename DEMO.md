# Demo Script for Judges

Use this flow to pitch and demo SupportFlow AI within a 3-5 minute timeframe.

## 1. Introduction (30 seconds)
"Hello judges, we built **SupportFlow AI**, an autonomous enterprise resolution engine. Today, customer support agents drown in hundreds of repetitive tickets, while customers wait hours for simple answers. SupportFlow solves this by instantly analyzing, routing, and directly resolving issues natively powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash."

## 2. Smart Ticket Creation Walkthrough (1 minute)
- **Go to "Tickets" page.**
- **Action**: Click "Create Ticket".
- **Concept**: "Instead of a customer manually filling out categories, priorities, and dropdowns, they just dump their email or frustrated message here."
- **Action**: Paste a dramatic complaint email. (e.g. "I have been waiting 4 days, my package is missing, refund me now or I'll sue").
- **Action**: Click "Analyze with SupportFlow AI".
- **Result**: Show the UI dynamically parse the Priority to "High", Sentiment to "Negative", Category to "Refund/Delivery", and generate a one-sentence summary.
- **Talking Point**: "Gemini structured this unstructured frustration into exact database columns instantly."

## 3. Multilingual Voice Assistant (1.5 minutes)
- **Go to "AI Assistant (Sandbox)" page.**
- **Action**: Turn ON Voice using the top-right toggle.
- **Concept**: "Not everyone types English fluently. Our engine supports multi-languages directly via voice."
- **Action**: Speak or Type in Hindi or Spanish: *“Mujhe apne subscription ka refund chahiye, kya policy hai?”* (I need a refund for my subscription, what is the policy?).
- **Result**: The agent evaluates the query against the *Knowledge Base integration* and responds *in Hindi*, using Voice Synthesis to output the response.
- **Talking Point**: "It detected the language, queried the knowledge base for refund rules (14-day policy), and responded natively without explicit translation steps. True inclusivity."

## 4. Enterprise Analytics (45 seconds)
- **Go to "Analytics Dashboard"**.
- **Action**: Show the graphs.
- **Concept**: "We don't just solve tickets, we give management the big picture."
- **Talking Point**: Highlight the **AI vs Human Resolution Rate** graph and the **Sentiment Trends**. 
- **Action**: Point to the "Action Hub"
- **Talking Point**: "Managers can instantly click to review negative cases or see immediately where the AI failed to resolve queries, allowing them to upload a new PDF to the Knowledge Base Manager."

## 5. Conclusion (15 Seconds)
"SupportFlow AI isn't a wrapper; it's a structural rewrite of how enterprises treat support routing and resolution. Built to scale, built to understand. Thank you."
